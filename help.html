How to Navigate the Website

Use the navigation bar at the top of each page to switch between the Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, and About pages.

Using the Booking Form

  • Fill in your name and select your major from the dropdown.
  • Choose your gender and select any hobbies you like.
  • Write additional comments in the provided text area.
  • Click the Submit button to send your booking.

Viewing Images and Data

The Gallery page contains three web-optimized images. Hover over the images to see a zoom effect.

You’ll also find a data table showing sample student information. The table includes headers, body rows, and a footer row.
